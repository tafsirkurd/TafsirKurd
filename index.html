About Tafsir Kurd

Our Sacred Mission

وَمَنْ أَحْسَنُ قَوْلًا مِّمَّن دَعَا إِلَى اللَّهِ وَعَمِلَ صَالِحًا وَقَالَ إِنَّنِي مِنَ الْمُسْلِمِينَ
"And who is better in speech than one who invites to Allah and does righteousness and says, 'Indeed, I am of the Muslims.'"
— Surah Fussilat (41:33)

At Tafsir Kurd, we embark on a sacred journey to illuminate the divine guidance of the Qur'an for Kurdish hearts and minds. We believe that the Qur'an is Allah's eternal miracle, a book that speaks to every generation, every culture, and every soul seeking truth. Our mission transcends mere translation—we strive to be bridges between the classical treasures of Islamic scholarship and the modern Kurdish community, ensuring that the profound wisdom of the Qur'an reaches every Kurdish home with the respect, clarity, and reverence it deserves.

"We follow the footsteps of the righteous scholars who devoted their lives to explaining Allah's Book, seeking only His pleasure and the guidance of His servants."

Our Guiding Principles

الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَتَطْمَئِنُّ قُلُوبُهُم بِذِكْرِ اللَّهِ ۗ أَلَا بِذِكْرِ اللَّهِ تَطْمَئِنُّ الْقُلُوبُ
"Those who believe and whose hearts are assured by the remembrance of Allah. Unquestionably, by the remembrance of Allah hearts are assured."
— Surah Ar-Ra'd (13:28)

Authenticity (الأصالة): Every explanation we offer is rooted in the authentic teachings of Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) and the interpretations of the righteous scholars (السلف الصالح). We reference trusted classical tafsir works like Ibn Kathir, At-Tabari, Al-Qurtubi, and others who dedicated their lives to understanding Allah's Book.

Clarity (الوضوح): We believe that the Qur'an's message should be crystal clear. Our content breaks down complex theological concepts into understandable Kurdish, making the profound accessible without losing its depth. We prioritize practical application over theoretical complexity.

Accessibility (السهولة): In this digital age, we utilize modern platforms to reach Kurdish speakers wherever they are. Short vertical clips serve those seeking quick daily reminders, while longer sessions nourish souls hungry for deeper understanding.

Sincerity (الإخلاص): This blessed work is performed purely for Allah's pleasure (لوجه الله الكريم). We seek no worldly recognition or personal gain, only the hope that Allah will accept our efforts and make them a means of guidance for His servants.

Respect for Scholarship (احترام العلماء): We stand humbly on the shoulders of giants—the great scholars who preserved and explained the Qur'an throughout Islamic history. Their wisdom guides our understanding and keeps us connected to the authentic Islamic tradition.

A humble servant of Allah (SWT), dedicated to sharing the beautiful teachings of Islam through the Kurdish language. With deep love for the Qur'an and unwavering commitment to the Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ), Saman strives to be a bridge connecting Kurdish hearts to the divine guidance of Allah's Book. Through years of study and devotion, he has made it his life's mission to make Islamic knowledge accessible, authentic, and meaningful for Kurdish-speaking communities worldwide.

إِنْ أُرِيدُ إِلَّا الْإِصْلَاحَ مَا اسْتَطَعْتُ ۚ وَمَا تَوْفِيقِي إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ

"I seek only to reform what I am able to reform. My success comes only from Allah."

— Qur'an 11:88

رَبَّنَا تَقَبَّلْ مِنَّا ۖ إِنَّكَ أَنتَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ
"Our Lord, accept from us. Indeed, You are the Hearing, the Knowing."
— Surah Al-Baqarah (2:127)

Personal Commitment: Every word spoken, every video created, every moment dedicated to this work is an act of worship and devotion to Allah (عز وجل). Following the complete Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ), sharing authentic Hadith stories, and striving to be among those who call people to Allah with wisdom and beautiful preaching (ادْعُ إِلَىٰ سَبِيلِ رَبِّكَ بِالْحِكْمَةِ وَالْمَوْعِظَةِ الْحَسَنَةِ).

Du'a for Our Viewers: May Allah (SWT) bless all who seek His guidance through His Book, make us among the people of the Qur'an (أهل القرآن), and grant us understanding, practice, and steadfastness upon His straight path. Ameen.
