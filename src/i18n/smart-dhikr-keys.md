# Smart Dhikr Keys — `gencine.smart.*`

---

## Daily / Time-based Hints

### `gencine.smart.morning_hint`
- **Kurdish:** ڕۆژا خوه ب زکرێ دەستپێکە
- **Shows:** Every morning
- **Means:** "Start your day with dhikr"

### `gencine.smart.waking_hint`
- **Kurdish:** دوای هاتنا خووێ بخوێنە
- **Shows:** Right after waking up
- **Means:** "Read after waking up" — morning wake-up dhikr

### `gencine.smart.sunrise_hint`
- **Kurdish:** کاتا نوێژا هەتاوهاتن
- **Shows:** Sunrise prayer time (Duha)
- **Means:** "Time of the sunrise prayer"

### `gencine.smart.evening_hint`
- **Kurdish:** ئێوارا خوە ب زکرێ بکە
- **Shows:** Evening time
- **Means:** "Make your evening with dhikr"

### `gencine.smart.sleep_hint`
- **Kurdish:** پێش خەوکردنێ بخوێنە
- **Shows:** Bedtime
- **Means:** "Read before sleeping"
