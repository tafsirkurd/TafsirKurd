### Rules Channel Template

**Server Rules:**
```
📜 **TAFSIR KURD SERVER RULES**

**1. ☪️ Islamic Conduct**
• Maintain Islamic etiquette and respect
• No disrespectful speech about Islam, Quran, or Prophets
• Keep discussions respectful and beneficial

**2. 🤝 Respect Everyone**
• Be kind and respectful to all members
• No harassment, bullying, or hate speech
• Respect different opinions and interpretations

**3. 🌍 Language**
• Kurdish (Badini/Sorani) and English are welcome
• Keep discussions in appropriate language channels
• Help non-native speakers when needed

**4. 📝 Stay On Topic**
• Use channels for their intended purpose
• Keep discussions relevant
• No spam or excessive self-promotion

**5. 🚫 No NSFW Content**
• Keep all content family-friendly
• No inappropriate images or discussions
• Maintain a clean Islamic environment

**6. 🔒 Privacy**
• Don't share personal information
• Respect others' privacy
• No doxxing or sharing private messages

**7. 💬 No Politics or Sectarianism**
• Focus on Quran and Islamic knowledge
• Avoid political debates
• No sectarian arguments

**8. 🤖 Bot Usage**
• Use bots in designated channels
• Don't spam bot commands
• Report bot issues to admins

**9. ⚠️ Violations**
• First offense: Warning
• Second offense: Timeout
• Third offense: Kick/Ban

**10. 📢 Listen to Moderators**
• Respect admin and moderator decisions
• Contact admins if you have concerns
• Appeals can be made via DM

---

By participating in this server, you agree to follow these rules and the Discord Terms of Service.

May Allah guide us all! 🤲
```

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Slow Mode:** Enable 5-10 second slow mode in busy channels to prevent spam

2. **Verification Level:** Set to Medium to prevent raids

3. **Content Filter:** Enable explicit content filter

4. **Welcome Screen:** Create an attractive welcome screen with key channels

5. **Channel Topics:** Add descriptive topics to each channel

6. **Pinned Messages:** Pin important info in each channel

7. **Regular Events:** Host weekly Quran study sessions

8. **Nitro Boost Perks:** Encourage boosting for better emojis, banner, audio quality
